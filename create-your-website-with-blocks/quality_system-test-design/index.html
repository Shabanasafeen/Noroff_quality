The quality System

The quality system shall ensure that the educations at Noroff are systematically evaluated and maintain high quality.

The quality system includes all education at Noroff: Bachelor, master’s and PhD educations as well as continuing and further education. The system includes the entire student journey from admission to completed education, as well as framework factors.

The quality system has the following main functions:

Slide 1

To promote quality in existing and planned educational offerings and ensure that students achieve a fixed learning outcome

Slide 2

To set a good framework, and support Noroff's main goal of being a leading provider of research and experience-based knowledge to the welfare society

Slide 3

To uncover and ensure follow-up of failing quality in the educations and to make visible and document the quality work at Noroff

Block
The reporting processes with responsibility and feedback loops at Noroff are summarized in the figure below. To ensure further development of the quality, the system is based on feedback loops. Assessment and analysis shall lead to follow-up and measures at the lowest possible level in the organization.

The board
-Is responsible for ensuring that the professional business maintains high quality.

-Provides management signals for priorities for the next period and ensures that budgetary priorities and other decisions facilitate good quality development of the university's study portfolio.
Institution
-The Rectorate has overall responsibility for the quality work at Noroff, and for the board to receive information about the study quality.

-The Rector is responsible for ensuring that the board's guidelines are followed up in the annual management dialogue meetings with the faculties.
Faculty
-The dean / center manager has overall strategic and coordinating responsibility for all development and R&D activities at the faculty / center.

-The dean / center manager is responsible for ensuring that the board's guidelines and the faculties' own action plans / measures to improve the quality of education are followed up.
Institute
-The head of department is responsible for quality assurance and development of the department's educations and overall portfolio.

-The head of department is responsible for following up the board's and faculty's guidelines and the department's own action plans in work with quality assurance and development of the department's study portfolio.
Program
-The study program manager has academic responsibility for the study program as a whole.

-The study program manager is responsible for following up the department's action plans in work with quality assurance and development of the study program.
Subject
-The course coordinator is responsible for planning, implementation and development of the course.

-The course coordinator is responsible for following up the course's and the department's action plans in work with quality assurance and development of the course.

Requirements and regulations

The Universities and University Colleges Act (the Universities and University Colleges Act) requires all universities and colleges to have a system for quality assurance that ensures and further develops the quality of the educations. 

In addition, there are a number of national and local requirements for and guidelines for quality work in the following external and internal regulations and management documents:

  • Regulations on quality assurance and quality development in higher education and vocational education (study quality regulations ) 
  • Regulations on supervision of the quality of education in higher education (the study supervision regulations ) 
  • Regulations on requirements for master’s degrees
  • Regulations on admission to higher education
  • The qualifications framework for higher education
  • Regulations on the degree of Ph.D. at Noroff – the metropolitan university
  • Regulations on studies and exams at Noroff – metropolitan university
  • Regulations on admission to studies at Noroff – metropolitan university
  • Strategy 2024
